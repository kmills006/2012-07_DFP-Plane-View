Details

Based on the Building Maps that can be located on Full Sail's Propeller site, Plane View uses an interactive approach to help students and faculty find their way around campus. The user has the option of using the search bar that will auto fill rooms as you type them out, or the option to click through the buildings to get to the specific room that they are trying to find.

Plane View will continue to be updated and refined in future builds based on the needs of the students and the faculty of Full Sail University.
