About

Plane View is an desktop application to help students and faculty at Full Sail University find their way around what can sometimes be a very confusing campus.

It brings the school to life in a whole new way. You can search for your upcoming classes, search for the location of different departments or just explore the campus through an interactive map.

Features

With Plane View you can either use the autofill search section to look up your classes or click on the specific buildings to find the class that you are looking for.

The departments button will show a list of the departments and other information including there location. Future iterations will include a key that will show outlet and lounge locations.
