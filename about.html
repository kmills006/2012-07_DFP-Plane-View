About Data Fire

Founded in July 2012, Data Fire is a new media company with a client-centric attitude. We use synergy and group collaboration to develop applications that go above and beyond the original expectations. Since we are a small company, we make sure that our clients are involved in every step of our building process so they can be involved in every step of the process. The success of each individual application is only determined by the satisfaction of the client.

Plane View

Plane View was initially conceived as a map application for the students of Full Sail University. The application maps out all of the class rooms, departments, and other amenities that students would use on a day-to-day basis.

What's Next

Data Fire will continue to create web applications and is slowly working to create mobile version for Plane View and will continue to make future iterations.
